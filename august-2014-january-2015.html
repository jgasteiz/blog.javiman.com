What I've been up to: Aug 2014 - Jan 2015

Mon 02 February 2015

Yes, I also thought this blog was dead. It's not even I don't have things to tell, I've actually done a lot of them during these last five months, I guess don't really have an excuse for not writing.

Remembering all the stuff will be easier with the help of some pictures, so here it goes.

September

The first half of September was so quiet, the second half was a complete madness to compensate. During exactly 2 weeks, I spent the first five days in Poland – my first Polish wedding – and the last ten in the USA, with Potato.

I don't have many pictures from the wedding, so I'll summarise it with these two:

There was half a litre of vodka per human being.

And because vodka was really good quality, this wasn't really necessary.

The Potato offsite, as it could be expected, went over all expectations. This year there was not only one destination, but three: Santa Monica, Las Vegas and Palm Springs, in that order. Notice how the first stop has quite a good balance between party possibilities, beach and relax. Vegas, a big rollercoaster in all imaginable ways. Palm Springs... god, what a nice way of ending this offsite.

October

Pretty much nothing here. The offsite left my brain half dead for 80% of the month and the only thing I remember from here is the Halloween pumpkins we carved.

December

Mostly, Christmas. It's just everywhere, there's no way of ignoring it. Also, I start getting my hands seriously dirty with Android and Arduino. I publish a couple of (shitty) apps in the Play Store and that makes me spend some extra time learning Android.

Pretty shite right now, but this app in particular stays as simple as I need it for my daily usage.

January

Back in Vitoria! For 10 days, time enough for going out every single day and wasting myself after some seriously inactive days in London. There's Jamón and Chorizo everywhere, porky paradise.

And as usual, I get to see all those people I normally don't see in my visits to Vitoria during the rest of the year.
